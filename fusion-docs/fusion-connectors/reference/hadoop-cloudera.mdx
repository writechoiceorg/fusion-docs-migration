Compatible with Fusion version: 4.0.0 through 4.2.6

The Cloudera Connector is a MapReduce-enabled crawler that is compatible with [Cloudera CDH v4.x and v5.x](http://www.cloudera.com/products/apache-hadoop/key-cdh-components.html).

<Note>

Deprecation and removal notice

This connector is deprecated as of Fusion 4.2 and is removed or expected to be removed as of Fusion 5.0.

For more information about deprecations and removals, including possible alternatives, see [Deprecations and Removals](/fusion-connectors/ft39rr/deprecations-and-removals).

</Note>
