permalink: "qxpasi"
---

As a search manager, search queries that generate zero results can drive customers away from the site. Experience Optimizer allows you to ensure this does not happen. Instead of display zero results, you can choose to display certain docuents when a customer reaches a zero results page.

Learn how to use the [optimizing zero results](/how-to/n8o6ui/eo-zero-results) feature.
