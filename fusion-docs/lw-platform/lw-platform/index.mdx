Lucidworks Platform features let you configure and manage applications, user accounts, integrations to Lucidworks Fusion and other software, and signals to generate helpful metrics.

The following topics provide detailed information about how to:

* [View and manage applications](/lw-platform/platform/scu2o5/products) to ensure optimal performance and usability.
* [Create and manage user accounts](/lw-platform/platform/00j8nx/users-and-provisioning), including roles and permissions that define access and the actions your employees can perform in the Lucidworks platform.
* [Integrate Lucidworks AI seamlessly with Lucidworks Fusion](/lw-platform/platform/mdlec0/lucidworks-ai-integrations) and other software to extend and enhance your workflows.
* View [related API information and access client credentials](/lw-platform/platform/oqzogo/ap-is-and-credentials), which are required to connect Lucidworks AI with your site.
* Configure [signals](/lw-platform/analytics/0le96f/signals), which are used to generate [metrics and analytics](/lw-platform/analytics/10aut8/lucidworks-analytics).
