## Get started with the Lucidworks Platform

  <Step title="Onboard your team">
    Implement SSO, invite users, define their roles, and grant access to the appropriate resources.

    <Frame caption="Invite and manage your users">

    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion title="SSO options" icon="lock">
        Workspace owners can configure SSO with [OpenID Connect (OIDC)](#oidc-authentication) or [Security Assertion Markup Language (SAML)](#saml-authentication).

        **OIDC**

        1. Add your team to Lucidworks Platform at **Settings > Users & Provisioning > Users**. These users must also exist in your OKTA instance.
        1. Create a new Okta application, using the OIDC sign-on method. 
        1. Create the idenity provider in Lucidworks Platform at **Settings > Users & Provisioning > Authentication > OIDC**. 

        **SAML**

        1. Add your team to Lucidworks Platform at **Settings > Users & Provisioning > Users**. These users must also exist in your OKTA instance.
        1. Create a new Okta application, using the SAML sign-on method. 
        1. Create the idenity provider in Lucidworks Platform at **Settings > Users & Provisioning > Authentication > SAML**.
